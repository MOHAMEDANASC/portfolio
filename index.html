About

A journey for career success.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Calicut University in 2023. After graduation, I realized I needed to develop specific skills, so I enrolled in a six-month SAP course. However, I wasn’t able to acquire the skills as effectively as I had hoped during this time. Meanwhile, I received a job offer from ICICI Bank.

My journey in the banking field has been challenging, as I struggled to meet my monthly targets. This created a lot of pressure, which affected my mental health. During this time, I learned about Brototype, where even students with only a high school education were securing well-paying jobs. This made me consider changing my career to the IT field for better career growth and a more competitive salary.

My hobbies include watching cricket, traveling, and reading books. My short-term goal is to complete the Brocamp program and secure a high-paying job in the IT field. In the long term, I aim to start a business in the medical industry.
